Creative Coding Lab Project B: Code + [ ]

Cell Division and Cycle

A cell is the most basic unit of life.

Cells go through a process called the Cell

Cycle in which they divide into mutliple cells.

Instructions:

For this project, use the mouse to click

anywhere on the p5.js sketch to have

cells appear and slowly grow large

and then fade.

You can also press the keyboard keys

a, s, or d to change the background picture.

When the a key is pressed,

the background will be a picture of a strawberry

under a microscope.

When the s key is pressed,

the background will be a picture of a celery

under a microscope.

When the d key is pressed,

the background will be a picture of an onion

under a microscope.

References:

Professor Moon's codes and

the p5.js reference (https://p5js.org/reference/)
